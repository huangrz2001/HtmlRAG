一、授权渠道 I. Licensed Channels​
1、被授权方有权在抖音电商全球购平台（域名：jinritemai.com，域名变更不影响本文件效力）使用授权品牌开设旗舰店，品牌使用范围包括店铺名称（具体以平台审核通过为准）、店铺LOGO、宣传素材等，并有权开展授权品牌相关商品的销售、推广活动；​
1. The licensee has the right to open a Flagship Store on the Douyin EC Global platform (domain name:jinritemai.com, changes to this domain name do not affect the validity of this document) using the Licensing Brand in its store name (subject to approval from the platform), store logo, marketing materials, etc. The licensee also has the right to conduct selling and marketing activities related to the Licensing Brand's products.​
​
2、被授权方有权在抖音平台（域名：douyin.com）使用授权品牌申请官方旗舰店企业号，或以授权品牌官方旗舰店作为其抖音企业号昵称并以官方账号作为认证信息，品牌使用范围包括昵称（具体以平台审核通过为准）、认证信息、账号头像等。​
2. The licensee has the right to apply for a Business Account of the Licensing Brand's Official Flagship Store or use "Official Flagship Store of <the Licensing Brand>" as the account name of its Douyin Business Account and use its official account as its authentication information on Douyin (domain name:douyin.com). The licensee can use the Licensing Brand in its account name, authentication information, account profile image, etc.​
​

II. Term of Validity of This License:___/___/_______-___/___/_______.​

特此授权。 III. Nature of This License: this is an exclusive license, which means within the term of validity of this License the licensor shall: 1) not open nor grant a license to any other company to open an Official Flagship Store of the Licensing Brand on the Douyin EC Global platform; 2) not apply for nor grant a license to any other company to apply for a certified Business Account of the Licensing Brand's Official Flagship Store on Douyin.​
The license is granted as above.​
